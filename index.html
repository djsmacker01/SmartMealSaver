Frequently Asked Questions

When will SmartMealSaver launch? +

SmartMealSaver is currently in private beta. Early access invites will begin rolling out in phases in 2026 to waitlist members based on location and usage needs. Joining the waitlist guarantees priority access before public release.

How much does it cost? +

SmartMealSaver will offer a free version with core features, alongside optional premium plans for advanced budgeting, deeper nutritional insights, and enhanced deal alerts. Final pricing will be shared with early access users before launch.

Which stores and platforms do you compare? +

We compare prices across major UK grocery retailers, local supermarkets, and popular takeaway platforms. Coverage will continue to expand as we integrate more partners and independent stores.

How accurate are the price comparisons? +

Prices are pulled in near real time and refreshed regularly. While availability and delivery fees may vary, SmartMealSaver is designed to show the most accurate and practical comparison possible at the moment you search.

Is my data private and secure? +

Yes. Your data is encrypted, never sold, and handled in full compliance with GDPR. You stay in control of what you share, and you can delete your data at any time.
